## Contact us!

You are always welcome to reach out to the Toit engineering team, if you are experiencing problems with setting up Toit.

We are ready to help you and we are available via the integrated chat, in our public [Slack](https://chat.toit.io) channel, or you can write us an email using developers@toit.io.
