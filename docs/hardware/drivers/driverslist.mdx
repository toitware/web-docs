# List of drivers

The list of drivers available in the [Toit library](https://libs.toit.io/drivers/library-summary) and on Github can be viewed here:


*Driver* | *Description* | *Source* | *Example*
  --- | --- | --- | ---
  `bma2x2` | Driver for Bosch accelerometers | [link](https://libs.toit.io/drivers/bma2x2/class-Bma2x2) |
  `bme280` | Driver for BME280 environmental sensor | [link](https://libs.toit.io/drivers/bme280/class-Bme280) |  [Weather station](https://github.com/toitware/bme280_post)
  `bmg160` | Driver for the gyroscope BMG160 | [link](https://libs.toit.io/drivers/bmg160/class-Bmg160) |
  `bmm050` | Driver for the magnetometer BMG160 | [link](https://libs.toit.io/drivers/bmm050/class-Bmm050) |
  `MAX31865` | Driver for MAX31865 RTD-to-Digital Converter | [link](https://github.com/toitware/max31865-driver) |  [SPI](https://github.com/toitware/max31865-driver/blob/main/examples/main.toit)
  `mcp342x` | Driver for MCP342x ADC devices |  [link](https://github.com/toitware/mcp342x-driver) |  [I2C](https://github.com/toitware/mcp342x-driver/blob/main/examples/main.toit)
  `icm20948` | Driver for ICM-20948 9-axis devices |  [link](https://github.com/toitware/icm20948-driver) |  [I2C](https://github.com/toitware/icm20948-driver/blob/main/examples/accel_gyro.toit)
